What We Stand For

Our purpose is to break the cycle of misinformation and give you the power to reclaim your digital and intellectual freedom.

Spreading Truth

We find and share the real story—what's happening now and what happened in the past. We help you see through the lies you are told every day.

Freedom Of Speech

We make sure everyone has a place to speak freely, without being silenced. We protect your right to share ideas, especially the ones they try to censor.

Protecting Online Privacy

We believe your private life should stay private. We build easy-to-use tools to stop big companies and the government from watching what you do online.
